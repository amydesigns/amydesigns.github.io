Swarm App: Club & Class Events Check-In

For the Fall 2018 Cornell AppDev Hackathon, my team and I created designed and created a semi-functional app in three weeks. I contributed to the project as its designer. I drafted sketches, wireframes, and rough prototypes, and determined the overall visual design. The end project can be found on github.

About "Swarm"

Our team noticed the struggle of Cornell students trying to balance all their class and club events. We decided to create an app that organizes users' school-related activities. While there are certainly many event apps, "Swarm" is distinguishable by its unique “Check-In” function. The iOS file can be found here and the Backend file can be found here.
